DUBROVNIK, 420 eur

DAY 1: DUBROVNIK
Group meeting in airport. Boarding and flight to Dubrovnik. Dubrovnik is the most famous and luxurious place in Croatia. Departure for hotel. Check in. Lunch. Free time. Dinner. Overnight stay.
DAY 2: DUBROVNIK [UNESCO-1979] – EXCURSION: CAVTAT
Breakfast in hotel. Dubrovnik sightseeing: Pile gate, Stradun walkway, Minčeta tower, city walls, Archlords palace, church of St. Vlaho – city saint and protector… Return to hotel for a lunch. In the afternoon, excursion to nearby town of Cavtat with lots of nice beaches. Free time. Return to hotel. Dinner. Overnight stay.
DAY 3: DUBROVNIK – EXCURSION: ELAFIT ISLANDS
Breakfast in hotel. An all day excursion trip on Elafit Islands by private boat with lunch on a boat (see fish menu). All day relaxation and see swiming… Return to hotel. Dinner. Overnight stay.
DAY 4: DUBROVNIK – EXCURSION: KORČULA
Breakfast in hotel. An all day excursion trip on Island of Korčula (by bus and ferry). Visit of town Korčula known for its middle age defence walls as well as for folklore dance called «Moreška» which represents middle age clashes with the Turks. Lunch on Korčula. Free time. Departure for ferry port in the late afternoon. Return to hotel. Dinner. Overnight stay.
DAY 5: DUBROVNIK
Breakfast in hotel. All day free time for individual programs, visits to museums, relaxation ext. Full board service in hotel. Lunch in hotel. Dinner in hotel. Overnight stay.
DAY 6: DUBROVNIK – AIRPORT
Breakfast in hotel. Check out, boarding luggage in buses. Free time in Dubrovnik until departure for airport. Arrival in airport. Passport check. Boarding on a plane. Ending of our services.
